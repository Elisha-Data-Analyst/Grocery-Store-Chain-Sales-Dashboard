\# ☕ Regional sales and store analytics Dashboard (Excel)



\## 📊 Project Overview

This project showcases an \*\*interactive Grocery Store Sales Dashboard\*\* built entirely using \*\*Microsoft Excel\*\*.  

The dashboard helps analyze sales performance, and product trends using real-world transactional data.



\## 🔍 Key Insights

&#x20;​

&#x20;- \*\*Suburbs are the top market:\*\* The suburban region stands out as the highest-

grossing area for total sales.

&#x20;- \*\*Sales are incredibly balanced across all stores:\*\* Even though Kosher is the highest-grossing store and Gourmet is the lowest, the gap between them is tiny (14M vs 13.2M). Every single store type is a major, consistent revenue generator.

&#x20; \*\*Huge overall numbers:\*\* Just combining these two formats brings in an incredible amount of money, proving that the business has strong, steady demand across the board rather than relying on just one star performer.

\## Technical Dashboard Specifications

\### \*\*Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)\*\*

High-level summary metrics that provide an immediate snapshot of organizational performance:

&#x20;\* \*\*Total Sales:\*\* Displays aggregate revenue generated across all operational touchpoints.

&#x20;\* \*\*Total Regions:\*\* Represents the geographic footprint and market scope of the dataset.

&#x20;\* \*\*Total Stores:\*\* Tracks the total number of active brick-and-mortar or digital retail locations.

\### \*\*Analytical Visualizations\*\*

Targeted charts focused on identifying top performance drivers:

&#x20;\* \*\*Highest Grossing Region (Chart):\*\* A dynamic visual identifying the top-performing geographic segment by revenue distribution.

&#x20;\* \*\*Highest Grossing Store (Chart):\*\* A granular visual breakdown highlighting individual high-volume retail locations driving the most sales.
